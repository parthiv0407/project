Admissiom Procedure


Upon recommendations of University Grants Commission (UGC), New Delhi and the State Government, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad has conferred Autonomous Status to the Institute from Academic year 2016-17, whereby the College will award Degree on behalf of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad. Admissions to the Institute are made along with the other engineering colleges in the state through a common entrance test conducted by the Govt. of Telangana.

THE IARE ADVANTAGE



Institute of Aeronautical Engineering (IARE), Hyderabad was established in the year 2000 by a devoted group of eminent professional and industrialists having a long and outstanding experience in educational system with a mission ‘Education for Liberation’. It is the first institute to start B.Tech program in Aeronautical Engineering in the state of Telangana and has gradually transformed itself into a integrated multi-disciplinary technological institute.

>


IARE is a prestigious Autonomous engineering college offering eleven B.Tech degree programs in Computer Science and Engineering, CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning), CSE (Data Science), CSE (Cyber Security), Computer Science and Information Technology, Information Technology, ECE, EEE, Aeronautical, Mechanical and Civil, six M.Tech degree programs and MBA with 21 years of rich standing in the educational era.

>


Founded In 2000, today IARE has a total student strength is 4952 with faculty strength of 272, out of which, 82 are Ph.D (30%) giving rise to healthy faculty student ratio.
Ranked in the top 3% of engineering colleges nationally with strong industry connects provide opportunities for every individual to achieve success on their own terms, and prepare them not just for a career, but for life.
Dedicated faculty offer students an innovative and stimulating learning experience both inside the classroom and laboratory environment and within their community.
Professional and support staff offers students every opportunity to succeed in their studies and extracurricular activities with specialized services tailored to their individual needs.



Superior facilities are available to students, faculty and staff, from air-conditioned smart classrooms and labs to business innovation to sports installations and to good food service in cafeteria.
Big placements greet our students with ample opportunities with around 36 core and software companies visiting the institute every year and successful in placing 76% of eligible students.
Research initiatives among students and faculty members and Entrepreneurship drive among students, makes IARE as one of the best institutions to enhance the performance of the students.
